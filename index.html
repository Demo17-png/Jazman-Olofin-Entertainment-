Olumuyiwa Olofinkuade, Jazzman Olofin, is an Arts and Entertainment Consultant with over 20 years of active involvement in the Nigerian entertainment industry. From setting up the famous musical group X-Appeal to a successful musical career as a solo artiste, he has worked closely with reputable organizations like Corporate Pictures, Kennis Music, and Storm Records.

He has many music albums to his credit and has had successful collaborations with other successful artistes like Adewale Ayuba, Musiliu Haruna Ishola, Paul Play Dairo, Dare Art Alade, Sasha, Ruggedman, Julius Agwu, Blackky, GT the Guitar man, Naeto C, MI, Dagrin, amongst others. Some of his music hits like "Raise da Roof," "Shake Something," and "Eko Ile" remain some of his fans' favourites.

Muyiwa's passion for the indigenous reflects in his works as his music is a blend of the Yoruba language, English Language, as well as a mix of indigenous production flavours. He also writes plays for television and theatre production.

In addition to his commitment to the Nigerian entertainment industry, he is currently involved in initiatives to promote the indigenous strengths of Nigeria with a view to changing the prevailing narrative from negative to positive. Muyiwa is a native of Ikere, Ekiti State. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Philosophy from the University of Lagos.
